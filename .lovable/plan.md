

# Phase 1: Plug-and-Play AI Website Personalization Widget

## Overview
A modern, minimal computer/laptop sales page with 3 benefit-focused hero templates and a template switcher for previewing personalization options. The page will serve as the foundation for the AI personalization widget.

---

## Hero Section with Template Switcher

**Template Switcher Panel**
- Floating control panel to toggle between the 3 hero templates in real-time
- Visual indicator showing which template is currently active

**3 Benefit-Focused Hero Templates**

| Template | Focus | Headline Style | Primary CTA | Hero Image |
|----------|-------|---------------|-------------|------------|
| **Power** | Performance & Speed | "Unleash Maximum Performance" | Shop Now | Gaming/high-performance imagery |
| **Value** | Affordability & Deals | "Premium Quality, Smart Price" | Explore Deals | Budget-friendly imagery |
| **Versatility** | Flexibility & Use Cases | "One Laptop, Endless Possibilities" | Learn More | Multi-use/lifestyle imagery |

Each template uses the same layout but swaps:
- Hero image
- Headline & subheadline text
- CTA button text and style

---

## Page Sections

**Featured Products Grid**
- 4-6 laptop cards with image, name, key specs, and price
- Clean grid layout with hover effects

**Social Proof & Reviews**
- Customer testimonials with ratings
- Trust badges (warranties, shipping, support)

**Features & Benefits**
- Icon-based feature highlights (performance, battery, display, etc.)
- Clean 3-4 column layout

**Newsletter & Contact Form**
- Email signup for deals and updates
- Simple, minimal form design
